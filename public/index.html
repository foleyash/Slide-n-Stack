Welcome to Slide-n-Stack!

Your goal is to stack the moving platforms as high as you possibly can. To drop a stack, press the space key if using a keyboard or tap the screen if on a mobile device.

As you progress through each level, the platform will move increasingly faster, so be ready to move quick, and, of course, have fun!
